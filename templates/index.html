Welcome to Marco Polo

What is MarcoPolo?

We as a team have designed this website in order for incoming freshman students to get familiar with what San Marcos has or has around to offer. The site contains information regarding standout resturants, recreational facilities, photo locations and outlets. Please note that this site is a work in progress and many contain some bugs. We are sorry for any inconvience they may cause you, but we hope you will find this site useful!!

About Us

We are a team of first year students attending the Google CSSI program at Cal State San Marcos. Although new to web design, we decided to act on our ambitions and create a website that is something that could pass as the website for a legitmate company, while keeping in mind the constraints of our limited knowledge.
